PHDays 8 Standoff

A few hours ago the «Standoff» ended. This is a CTF competition which happens during the PHDays forum.

I played for the «Level 8» this year. Although some people think that CTFs are useless, I can’t agree with that. You can always learn something new and follow the way others think.

I exploited an interesting RCE in a .net app and hacked Telecom operator. That was fun!

Members of my team are great. I hope to see them again.
